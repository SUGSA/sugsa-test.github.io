Call for Papers

Numerous instances of the Regional Scrum Gathering®, supported by Scrum Alliance, take place around the Globe every year. South Africa’s event has been running for ten years. It enjoys a reputation for being an excellent opportunity to learn and to connect with the local Agile and Scrum community; making it a popular, highly anticipated event in the calendar.

The call for papers is now open and we are currently looking for speakers to present talks and workshops that welcome change in our community of Agilists.

If you are interested in being a speaker, please check out our Call for Papers information page to submit your inspirational talks aligned to our theme of Welcome Change.
